An ‘After School learning centre’ is an ideal & unique concept to provide Quality education along with moral values.

We provide scientific classes with the concept of ‘Pahle Dikhao – Fir Sikhao’. We have a large number of Science demo experiments using very low cost materials which is used in the classroom while teaching.. The demo based activities will bridge the gap between text book Physics and daily life Physics.

We not only provide the best in the academic curriculum but we dare to make them learn in every other fields of the life by various other activities like -


  • Encourage everyone (students, teachers, ourselves) to read books other than academics.“The more you read, the more things you will know. The more that you learn, the more places you'll go.” - Dr. Seuss

  • Organising reflection session on every Sunday to share what everyone is learning new & other than academics by reading books, meeting people, travelling etc. “Reading without reflecting is like eating without digesting.”- Edmund Burke

  • Organising talks/seminars/reflective sessions by IITians/ NITians/ Scientists/ Acadmecians/ Artists/ Social Workers which make them sensitive against environmental & social issues as well apart from building them academically strong.

We believe that Education is not that only happen in school or tuition centres, it’s has more deep meaning

Six of the students of Evening Learning Centre of the class IXth have been selected for Indian Institute of Science Education & Research (IISER) Bhopal Summer Camp – 2013. Asha belong to middle family background just after 10th went for polytechnic to SLIET, Punjab. Praveet, belong to a very rural background and low middle class family after continuous hard work & support cleared JEE main through Bihar Sharif only and joined NIT Jamshedpur.
